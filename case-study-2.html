Enhancing User Experience in a Mobility and Public Transportation Engineering Company

Overview

As a UI/UX Expert at Systra, an engineering firm specializing in mobility and public transportation, I had the opportunity to work on a diverse range of projects at various stages of product development. Depending on the product's lifecycle, the team and I employed a tailored approach to ensure the best outcomes. This involved conducting interviews, audits, and workshops to gather insights, understand user needs, and align design goals. By leveraging Material Design components from Vuetify, I focused on creating intuitive and efficient user interfaces across applications. Here are some of the tasks I worked on consistently throughout the projects:


✷ User Research: Conducted interviews, surveys, and usability tests to gain a deep understanding of user requirements, pain points, and expectations.


✷ Prototyping and Wireframing on Figma: Created UI mockups and interactive prototypes to visualize design concepts, gather feedback, and iterate on the interface.


✷ User Journeys: Established user journeys using tools like Miro to map out the desired workflow and identify areas for improvement.


✷ Design System Integration on Figma: Leveraged Material Design components from Vuetify to ensure visual consistency and familiarity across applications, enhancing the overall user experience.


✷ UX and Accessibility Audits: Conducted comprehensive audits to evaluate the usability and accessibility of the applications, identifying areas for improvement and ensuring compliance with accessibility guidelines.


✷ Requirements Gathering and Evaluation: Collaborated with stakeholders to gather user requirements, translate them into actionable design features, and evaluate design solutions against those requirements.


✷ Documentation and Design System Maintenance: Maintained UI documentation on platforms like ZeroHeight, ensuring up-to-date resources for the design team and stakeholders. Contributed to updating and enhancing the design system components in Figma, promoting design consistency and efficiency.


✷ Iterative Design and User Feedback: Incorporated user feedback and conducted iterative design cycles to refine the interfaces, address usability issues, and optimize workflows.


✷ Layout Adjustments: Made layout adjustments based on user feedback, improving information hierarchy, visual clarity, and overall usability.


By employing this comprehensive and adaptable approach, I consistently aimed to deliver user-centered and high-quality user experiences across the risk management web platform, design document verification web application, RFP web application, and climate prediction web application.

Projects

1. Risk Management Web Platform

Objective: streamline risk assessment processes for transportation projects.
Outcome: enhanced user experience, making it intuitive and efficient for risk management professionals. Improved workflows and reduced errors in risk assessment.

2. Design Document Verification Web Application

Objective: streamline the process of submitting and evaluating proposals for transportation projects.
Outcome: created an intuitive and efficient interface that improved the document verification workflow. Streamlined document verification processes and increased accuracy.

3. RFP Web Application

Objective: streamline the process of submitting and evaluating proposals for transportation projects.
Outcome: created an intuitive and efficient user interface that simplified complex RFP workflows. Improved proposal submission and evaluation processes, reducing time and effort.

4. Climate Prediction Web Application

Objective: provide users with accurate and visualized climate predictions for transportation planning.
Outcome: developed an engaging and informative user experience that effectively allowed users to gather climate data. Enabled users to make data-driven decisions for transportation planning and RFP proposals/ feasibility studies.

Conclusion:

At Systra, an engineering firm specializing in mobility and public transportation, I had the opportunity to work on various applications at different stages of product development. Depending on the product lifecycle and specific needs of each application, my team and I employed a user-centric approach that involved interviews, audits, workshops, and usability testing.

By extensively utilizing Material Design components from Vuetify, I developed UI mockups and prototypes, gathered user requirements, established user journeys, and updated the company's design system and UI documentation. Through iterative design processes and collaboration with stakeholders, we successfully improved the user experience of the risk management web platform, design document verification web application, RFP web application, and climate prediction web application.
