About me

Hello, I'm Maria Montagu, a front-end developer who enjoys creating attractive and user-friendly interfaces. I have a background in Marketing, communications, and customer success, which helps me take a user-focused approach to front-end development. I blend design and technology to make interfaces that not only meet users' needs but also go beyond their expectations.

I'm passionate about design and strive to create visually appealing and intuitive interfaces. I combine my technical skills with an eye for aesthetics to deliver seamless experiences that leave a strong impression.

Besides design, I enjoy staying updated with the latest industry trends and continuously learning. This allows me to bring new ideas and innovative solutions to each project I work on. I'm excited to collaborate with like-minded individuals and organizations to create exceptional digital experiences that truly connect with users.
